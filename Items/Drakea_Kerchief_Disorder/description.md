This handy-dandy, versatile garment can be worn around the neck, in the hair, or on your face if you want to cosplay as a cowboy robbing a bank. Can also be tied around your arms or legs, but only if you want to look like a moron

This simple, square piece of fabric is a reminder that looks can be deceiving. The wearer of this item receives the following benefits:

* +5 to the Detect Trap Skill.
* Wearer may cast a level-15 Tripper spell once every five hours.
* The Remote Detonator Benefit.
