# Breathing Skill

The most **fundamental** skill in the dungeon - the ability to breathe.

## Key Features:
- *Automatic* breathing process
- Works in most environments
- Essential for survival

> **Note**: This skill cannot be unlearned.

### Advanced Techniques:
1. **Deep breathing** - for stress relief
2. **Rapid breathing** - during combat
3. **Held breath** - for stealth situations

**Warning**: Do not attempt to hold your breath indefinitely!
